Illuminating Threats, Guiding Defence

We offer expertise in a number of cyber security domains, working to shine light on cyber threats, improve threat detection capabilities, and prepare businesses to effectively respond to potential cyber incidents. Our commitment is to improve the security of your technology, data and operations, increasing your businesses confidence in today's increasingly complex threat landscape.

Services

Threat Hunting

Our Threat Hunting service proactively searches your business for existing, hidden and emerging cyber threats using methods appropriate to your capabilities.


  • Threat Discovery: We utilise methods aligned with the MITRE ATT&CK framework to identify potential threats across available data sources and systems.

  • Review of Findings: The results are thoroughly reviewed for potential threats. This includes a study of the threat's source, nature, and its existing or potential impact.

  • Recommendations: We provide advice on enhancing your threat detection capabilities by utilising the latest technologies and best practices.

  • Final Report: You'll receive a report detailing identified threats or detection gaps, as well as our recommendations to help your team effectively detect and manage potential threats.

Threat Detection Engineering

Our Threat Detection Engineering service improves your business's situational awareness and threat detection capabilities so that you can better identify threats targeting your business.


  • Data Review: We examine the data you're currently collecting and review it's suitably for identifying threats relevant to your business.

  • Alert System Review: We assess how your existing alert systems operate and integrate into other systems to verify their effectiveness in detecting potential cyber threats.

  • Threat Monitoring Review: We analyse how the business currently identifies and responds to threats, assessing the end-to-end effectiveness.

  • Recommendations: We provide advice on improving your data collection, alert configuration, and threat monitoring processes based on industry best practices.

  • Final Report: You'll receive a report detailing our analysis, findings, and actionable recommendations, enabling you to better understand how to protect your business from threats.

Cyber Incident Preparedness

Our Cyber Incident Preparedness service helps prepare your business to respond more effectively to cyber threats, thereby giving you greater confidence in the event of an incident.


  • Process and Playbook Review: We examine your current capabilities and processes for responding to cyber threats to determine their effectiveness.

  • Technical Action Guide Review: We assess the effectiveness and integration of existing playbooks and technical guides.

  • Recommendations: We offer advice on improving your processes, playbooks, technical guides, and integrations in line with industry best practices.

  • Final Report: You'll receive a report detailing our analysis, findings, and actionable recommendations, helping improve your business's response capabilities against cyber threats.
